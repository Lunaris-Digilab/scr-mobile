Section 01

Overview

Welcome to Glowist ("we," "our," or "us"). Glowist is a skincare routine tracker application available on Android and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Glowist mobile application and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

By downloading, installing, or using Glowist, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Service.

Our commitment: We only collect information that is necessary to provide and improve the Glowist experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Section 02

Data We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you use the Service. This includes information you provide directly, information generated automatically through your use of the app, and, in some cases, information from third-party sources.

Category Examples Purpose
Account Information Email address, display name, profile photo Create and manage your account
Skin Profile Skin type, concerns (acne, dryness, etc.), allergies, climate Personalize your routine recommendations
Routine Data Products added, steps completed, streak counts, routine schedules Core app functionality and progress tracking
Product Data Product names, brands, categories, expiration dates you enter Manage your product shelf
Usage Analytics Features used, session duration, app performance data Improve app stability and user experience
Device Information Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, language settings Troubleshooting and compatibility
Notification Preferences Reminder times, notification opt-in/out status Send daily routine reminders

We do not collect sensitive health or medical data, payment information (the app is free), location data, or contacts from your device.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Service: Deliver core features such as routine tracking, product shelf management, streak counting, and progress visualization.
  • To personalize your experience: Use your skin profile to tailor content, recommendations, and in-app suggestions.
  • To send notifications: Deliver daily AM/PM routine reminders and milestone celebration alerts (only with your permission).
  • To improve the app: Analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to fix bugs, improve features, and optimize performance.
  • To communicate with you: Respond to support requests, send important service updates, and notify you of changes to this policy.
  • To ensure security: Detect fraudulent activity, prevent abuse, and protect the integrity of our Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations: Meet applicable data protection and regulatory requirements.

We do not use your data for automated profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects, nor do we use your data to serve third-party advertisements within the app.

Section 04

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting). These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only to perform services on our behalf and to protect it appropriately.
  • Authentication Partners: If you sign in with Google or Apple, those platforms handle authentication. Please review their respective privacy policies for details on how they process your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice in the app if such a transfer occurs.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

We never sell your data. Your personal information is not a product. Glowist's business model does not involve monetizing user data with advertisers or data brokers.

Section 05

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Specifically:

  • Active accounts: Your routine data, skin profile, and product shelf are stored for the duration of your account's existence so you can access your history at any time.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account through the app, we permanently delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain information for legal compliance purposes.
  • Anonymized analytics: Aggregated, de-identified usage data may be retained indefinitely as it can no longer be linked to you personally.
  • Backup systems: Deleted data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being permanently purged.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the app's Settings > Account > Delete Account, or by contacting us at privacy@glowist.app.

Section 06

Security

Protecting your data is a priority for us. We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS (Transport Layer Security)
  • Encryption of data at rest in our cloud infrastructure
  • Secure authentication options including Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In with OAuth 2.0
  • Strict access controls — only authorized personnel can access user data, on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing

While we take these steps to safeguard your information, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use the Service at your own risk. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

Section 07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Object to certain types of processing, including for direct marketing.

Opt-Out of Notifications

Disable push notifications at any time via app settings or device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@glowist.app. We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

Glowist is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below these age thresholds.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us at privacy@glowist.app. We will promptly investigate and, if confirmed, delete any such information from our systems.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under the applicable age of consent, we will take immediate steps to delete that data.

Section 09

Third-Party Services

Glowist integrates with certain third-party services to provide core functionality. Each of these services has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them:

  • Google Sign-In: Used for optional authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign-In: Used for optional authentication on iOS. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for backend infrastructure, authentication, and crash reporting. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics Provider: We use a privacy-respecting analytics solution to collect anonymized, aggregated app usage data. No personally identifiable information is shared.

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, and this Privacy Policy does not apply to them.
